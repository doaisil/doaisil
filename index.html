UK EPR Rules Highlight the Data Management Challenge

From Policy to Practicality: The Data Burden

New guidance from the UK government on Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) for packaging lays bare the immense data management challenge facing producers. The rules require organizations to collect and report highly granular data, including not just material and weight, but also packaging activity (e.g., importing, branding), packaging type (household vs. non-household), and packaging class (primary, secondary, tertiary).

Compliance Creates a Software Imperative

This level of detail makes manual tracking via spreadsheets virtually impossible and highly prone to error. As these regulations come into full force in 2025 (based on 2024 data), affected companies will have a clear and urgent need for a robust software solution to manage data collection, aggregation, and reporting to avoid significant financial penalties. This shifts the software purchasing decision from an operational efficiency choice to a core compliance necessity.
